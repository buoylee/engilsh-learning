🚩 【英文完整 5 分钟对话】

You: Is there a place in Australia you’ve always wanted to visit but haven’t yet?

Them: Oh, that’s a good question. I’ve always wanted to visit Tasmania, but I haven’t had the chance yet.

You: Tasmania? I’ve heard it’s beautiful! What’s so special about it for you?

Them: I’ve seen photos of the mountains, lakes, and all the wildlife there. It seems like a peaceful place to hike and relax, away from the busy city.

You: That sounds amazing. I would love to see the Tasmanian devils there too!

Them: Yes, exactly! And I’ve heard the air is really fresh, and the food, especially the seafood, is excellent.

You: I love seafood! Do you like hiking as well?

Them: Yes, I try to go hiking whenever I can. It helps me clear my mind after a busy week of work.

You: That’s a great habit. I also find walking in nature really helps me relax.

Them: Absolutely. How about you? Is there a place in Australia you’re looking forward to visiting during your trip?

You: I really want to visit the Great Barrier Reef and maybe go diving if I can. It’s been on my list for a long time.

Them: Oh, that would be incredible! Just remember to check the weather and book in advance.

You: Thanks for the tip! By the way, have you done diving before?

Them: Just once, but it was in Queensland. It was a beautiful experience, seeing all the colorful fish and coral.

You: That sounds awesome. I hope I can try it too. If you ever get to Tasmania, let me know how it is!

Them: Sure, and if you get to the Reef, I’d love to hear about your experience too!

🚩 练习稿 1：交通 - 公交与地铁

You: How do people usually get around in this city?
Them: Most people take the train or buses. The public transport is quite reliable.
You: That’s good to know. Do you use a transport card here?
Them: Yes, we use the Opal card in Sydney. It’s very convenient.
You: Oh, I’ve heard about it. Is it easy to top up?
Them: Yes, you can top it up at stations or online.
You: Great, I think I will get one when I arrive.
Them: You definitely should, it saves money too.

中文辅助理解
	•	你：人们在这里通常怎么出行？
	•	对方：大多数人乘坐火车或公交，公共交通很可靠。
	•	你：听起来不错，这里是用交通卡吗？
	•	对方：是的，在悉尼用 Opal 卡，非常方便。
	•	你：我听说过它，充值方便吗？
	•	对方：是的，在车站或网上都能充值。
	•	你：太好了，到时我也办一张。
	•	对方：一定要办，这样也能省钱。
